The Best Movies According to Obinna Chukwuma Nwosu

My top three movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite movie. I love the images and it feels like real live events.

Ex Machina

This one is a bluckbuster movie ever produced by Nollywood.
It paints the African imagery and poetery like never seen before.
The movie is a gathering of the most talented actors and actresses in the continent of Africa.

Drive

Beautiful film, really artistic. Every lover of the green revolution must see this movie.
I was held spell bound from the begining of the movie to the end.
